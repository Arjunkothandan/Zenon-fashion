About Zenon

Your simple and smart online shopping experience

What is Zenon?

Zenon is a beginner-level e-commerce website inspired by Amazon, created purely for learning and educational purposes. This project helps in understanding how online shopping platforms work in real-world scenarios.

Our Purpose

The main goal of Zenon is to gain hands-on experience in web development by implementing features such as product listing, categories, user authentication, and basic cart functionality.

What You Can Do

  • Browse products by category
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Add products to a shopping cart
  • Understand the checkout workflow
